Travel OF KABUL AFGHANISTAN

Kabul is the capital and largest city of Afghanistan, located in the eastern part of the country.
It is one of the oldest cities in the region, with a rich history and cultural significance.
Here's some information about Kabul:

History

Kabul has a history dating back over 3,500 years, with evidence of settlement in the area from ancient times. The city has served as a significant center for trade, culture, and politics, and it was an important stop on the Silk Road. Kabul has experienced periods of both growth and conflict, with invasions, wars, and changes of power over the centuries.

Kabul University, often referred to as Kabul University or Kabul Uni, is the oldest and one of the most prestigious universities in Afghanistan.

Kabul University was founded in 1932 as part of King Amanullah Khan's modernization efforts in Afghanistan. Over the years, it has played a significant role in higher education and research in the country.
